Dinger

Dinger is a smart restaurant paging system designed to streamline food pickup, minimize queues, and improve customer satisfaction. It allows customers to register their ticket numbers via their phones and get notified instantly when their food is ready.

Key Features

  • Customer ticket registration via phone or QR code
  • Real-time dashboard for restaurant staff
  • Ready, Preparing, and Completed food tracking
  • Automated notifications when food is ready
  • Future-ready display screen integration
